## General Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is Cerevox?">
    Cerevox is a powerful research assistant designed to help you analyze and extract insights from large datasets across multiple file types. It enables you to efficiently search through your documents while providing verifiable, source-backed answers to your queries. Whether you're working with PDFs, spreadsheets, or legal documents, Cerevox ensures every response is accurate and traceable to its source.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How can Cerevox benefit my business?">
    Cerevox streamlines your research and analysis workflows by enabling you to:
    - Process and analyze large volumes of documents efficiently
    - Work with multiple file types in a single platform
    - Get answers with precise source citations
    - Perform cross-document analysis
    - Save time on data searching and verification
    This allows your team to focus on analysis and decision-making rather than searching for information.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What file types does Cerevox support?">
    Cerevox supports a wide range of text-based formats including:
    - PDF, TXT, CSV
    - DOCX, PPTX, XLSX
    - Google Docs, Google Slides, Google Sheets
    - Parquet and JSON files
    We can efficiently process these files regardless of size, handling documents from a few KB to multiple TBs.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Use Cases

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How can Cerevox help with financial analysis?">
    For financial analysis, Cerevox enables you to efficiently search and analyze 10-K filings, annual reports, and financial statements. You can extract key insights from both tables and text, with all information properly cited to its source. This makes it easier to compile financial data, track metrics across documents, and verify information for reporting.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can Cerevox assist with legal research?">
    Yes, Cerevox is particularly valuable for legal research. It allows you to quickly analyze large sets of legal discovery documents, contracts, and case files while maintaining clear source citations. This ensures that every finding is traceable and verifiable, which is crucial for legal work. The platform's ability to search across multiple documents simultaneously significantly speeds up the research process.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How does Cerevox support enterprise knowledge management?">
    Cerevox helps organizations better manage their internal knowledge by enabling efficient search and sourcing of answers from internal documents, manuals, and policy archives. The platform's ability to handle diverse file types and provide accurate citations makes it easier to maintain and access institutional knowledge. This is particularly valuable for large organizations with extensive documentation spread across multiple formats and locations.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
