## General Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is Cerevox?">
    Cerevox is an data normalization platform for AI developers and businesses. AI/ML technologies can only be properly leveraged with structured data. Cerevox normalizes any data to your desired structure so that it can be used with AI/ML models and frameworks. Cerevox provides hosted APIs, libraries, and a CLI to streamline data normalization for AI development.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How can Cerevox benefit my business?">
    Cerevox simplifies AI development by offering managed solutions, enabling you to focus on innovation rather than infrastructure management. We enhance performance, scalability, and security of your infrastructure while reducing your operational overhead.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What programming languages does Cerevox support?">
    Cerevox supports Python and Node.js for easy integration into your projects. Our versatile CLI caters to developers across different technology stacks
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
