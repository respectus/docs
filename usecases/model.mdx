Data science workflows often require transforming unstructured text, such as raw reports or data tables, into structured datasets for machine learning models. Cerevox helps data scientists automate this process by extracting valuable structured insights from various document types. With the ability to handle diverse data sources, Cerevox makes it easy to prepare data for AI model training, ensuring that your models are powered by high-quality, relevant information. Whether you're cleaning data, feature engineering, or preparing training sets, Cerevox accelerates your data preparation tasks, saving time and increasing model accuracy.
